INTERNET OF THINGS

What is Internet Of Things?

The Internet of Things (IoT) is the concept of linking any gadget to the Internet and other connected devices (as long as it has an on/off switch). The Internet of Things (IoT) is a massive network of interconnected things and people that all collect and share data on how they are utilized and the world around them.

HOW DOES IoT IMPACT OUR LIVES?

FOR ENERGY CONSUMPTION

Anything that will help us lessen the amount of stress we put on the environment should be welcomed. Household appliances with high energy usage will change depending on dynamic pricing signals to reduce your power cost. Your home's lighting and thermostats will be able to learn your specific patterns in order to produce the best possible environment based on your daily routine.

FOR HEALTHCARE

IoT will grow into solutions where wearable technologies may assist cut costs and increase security and quality in patient care, particularly in the health and hospital industry. IoT technologies will enable advanced control of hospital resources, process improvement in patient care, and smart asset management via data-capturing sensors and RFID chips.

FOR DRIVING AND TRAFFIC

When an ambulance or other emergency vehicle approaches, traffic signals will be able to react to real-time traffic circumstances. Furthermore, road sensors will be able to adjust the speed limit based on weather conditions and previous incidents, as well as communicate directly with the car dashboard about dangerous situations with the use of IoT.

Best Application of IoT

Smart Home Security

With IoT we can easily view or access the security of our homes especially with the use of smart cameras.

Smart Glasses

Smart glasses are basically wearable phones which gives users easy access and control to their phone without touching it.

Fitness Tracker Bands

Trackers are one of the very useful applications of IoT which helps users track not only their activity but also their health.
