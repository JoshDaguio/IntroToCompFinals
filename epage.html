ELECTRONIC DEVICES

What are Electronic Devices?

Electronic devices are components that govern the passage of electrical currents in order to process data and control systems. Transistors and diodes are two prominent examples. Electronic devices are typically tiny and can be packaged together into integrated circuits. The present electronics boom is based on miniaturization.

How Does Electronic Devices Improve Our Lives?

FOR EDUCATION

Usage of technology in classrooms imporves engagement and knowledge retention. It also encourages both individual learning and collaboration. Students and teachers could learn useful life skills through technology.

FOR THE FUTURE

Technology is always improving. New employment and sectors, such as coding and artificial intelligence, are created as a result. In AI, IT, design, and many other STEM professions, technology provides a makers education. All of this is advantageous because AI is expected to replace 40% of occupations in the future.

FOR SAFETY

Mobile apps, wearables, machine sensors, cloud-based software, predictive analytics, 3D printing, robots, real-time personnel monitoring and tracking, PPE tracking with embedded sensors, and other emerging technologies are all being applied to workplace safety.
